Frequently Asked Questions

What conditions are covered? ▼

All our plans cover 100+ critical illnesses including all major types of cancer, heart disease, kidney failure, and more. Each plan has specific coverage details outlined in the policy documents.

Is there a waiting period? ▼

MediCare Plus offers lump sum payout with no waiting period. Other plans may have standard waiting periods as per policy terms. Please read the policy documents or contact us for details.

How do I claim? ▼

Claims can be filed through our online portal or by contacting our 24/7 support team. Most claims are processed within 7-10 business days. Required documents include medical reports and diagnosis confirmation.

Can I switch plans? ▼

Yes, you can switch between plans at any time. Your coverage will be adjusted accordingly, and there are no penalties for switching. Contact our team to discuss your options.

What about pre-existing conditions? ▼

Coverage for pre-existing conditions depends on the specific plan and policy terms. We recommend discussing your medical history with our team to find the best option for you.
